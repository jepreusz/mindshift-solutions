Migration Options

F5 recommends that customers who currently use Edge Gateway, use BIG-IP APM stand-alone platforms as an alternative. BIG-IP APM is a flexible, high-performance access and security solution that provides unified global access to your business-critical applications and networks.
Technical Support
F5 maintains generous lifecycle policies that allow customers to enjoy many years of both support and new software releases. Customers whose platforms are designated EoL and who are currently under maintenance contracts will continue to receive technical support until the expiration of the service contract renewal date.
