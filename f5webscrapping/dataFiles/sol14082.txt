


Known Issue

In rare cases, BIG-IP 8900/8950/8950S devices may stop responding or reboot with a Super I/O watchdog timeout error message. This issue is more likely to occur when the BIG-IP host is under high traffic load.


Impact

BIG-IP 8900/8950/8950S devices may experience temporary or permanent interruption of traffic.

Symptoms

As a result of this issue, you may encounter the following symptoms:

The BIG-IP host will stop responding or reboot due to a watchdog timeout.
The /var/log/ltm log file may display error messages that appear similar to the following examples:
 
 notice chmand[4601]: 012a0005:5: Host CPU subsystem reset - PCI reset asserted
 notice chmand[4601]: 012a0005:5: Host CPU subsystem reset caused by a Southbridge system reset
 warning chmand[4601]: 012a0004:4: Host CPU subsystem reset caused by *** Super I/O watchdog timeout ***
 info promptstatusd[4609]: 01460007:6: semaphore chmand.running(1) released
 info promptstatusd[4609]: 01460007:6: 
 info overdog[4610]: 01140100:6: Overdog daemon startup.
 notice overdog[4610]: 01140103:5: Watchdog touch enabled.


ResolutionStatusF5 Product Development has assigned ID 372170 to this issue, and has confirmed that this issue exists in the products listed in the Applies To box.
