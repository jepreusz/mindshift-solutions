


Known IssueDescription
Real-Time Streaming Protocol (RTSP) ALG connections do not have the data session translation address or ports logged as Large Scale NAT (LSN) translations.
This issue occurs when the following condition is met:


A carrier-grade network address translation (CGNAT) virtual server is associated with an RTSP ALG profile and an LSN pool.

Impact
Media flows cannot be traced to identify subscribers.
Symptoms
As a result of this issue, you may encounter the following symptoms:

BIG-IP iHealth  lists Heuristic H437215 on the Diagnostics > Identified > High screen.
Established RTSP media flows do not have their public translation address and ports logged as LSN translations.

F5 Product Development has assigned ID 431240 to this issue. F5 has confirmed that this issue exists in the products listed in the Applies To box, located in the upper-right corner of this article. For information about releases or hotfixes that resolve this issue, refer to the following table:
